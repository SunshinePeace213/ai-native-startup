# Locked Requirements — <Client Name>

The frozen discovery record. Each of the twelve dimensions carries a marker line of the
exact shape `- <dimension>: <state>` where `<state>` is:

- `open` — still gathering; not yet safe to build against.
- `locked` — the dimension's "locked when" criterion (see the cpo-question-bank skill) is
  met; the facts below are frozen and the PRD may cite them.

When a dimension locks, change its marker `<state>` to `locked` (for example
`- business & goals: locked`) and fill its content block with the agreed facts. Late
client answers that contradict a locked dimension re-open it and mark downstream stages
`stale`.
