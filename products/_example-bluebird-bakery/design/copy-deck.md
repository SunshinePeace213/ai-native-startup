# Copy Deck — Bluebird Bakery

Source: prd.md (content requirements) + section-briefs.md. Every text string the build
needs, one row per string, grouped by page and section. Source is `client-provided`,
`to-write`, or `placeholder`. Copy matches the PRD's content requirements and tone —
warm, homey, welcoming (never fancy) — and invents no claims beyond the PRD.

Notes for the reader:

- `to-write` strings below are **drafted, client-grade copy ready for Maria's review** (Soriza drafts, Maria approves for tone and accuracy). Nothing here is final until Maria signs off.
- `client-provided` marks facts Maria owns — the menu, exact hours, address, parking specifics, phone/email, the Google Business Profile URL, the Instagram handle, and the gallery photos. Shown here as clearly-labeled placeholders.
- `placeholder` marks strings that depend on final assets — image alt text and gallery captions get a drafted suggestion to be confirmed once photos are selected.
- Section keys follow the design-standard section catalog and reconcile with the forthcoming section-briefs.md (Jonas Weber, UX Designer). Navigation and Footer are global (one entry, used on every page).
